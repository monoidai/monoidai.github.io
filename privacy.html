5. Cookies

monoid.AI Enabled Sites use cookies to collect non-personal information including standard internet log information and details of Your behavioral patterns upon visiting a monoid.AI Enabled Site. This is done to enable the monoid.AI platform to deliver You with a better experience, identify preferences, diagnose technical problems, tailoring content and ads, analyze trends and generally improve eCommerce results.

Cookies are small data files transferred onto computers or devices by websites for record-keeping purposes and to enhance functionality on the monoid.AI Enabled Site.

Most browsers allow You to choose whether to accept cookies or not. If You do not wish to have cookies placed on Your computer, please set Your browser preferences to reject all cookies, before accessing any monoid.AI Enabled Site. Please note that by doing so, some features of certain monoid.AI Enabled Sites may become unavailable.

To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
